# VELUNE — Brand & Design Guidelines

## 1. Brand Essence

VELUNE is a minimal luxury skincare brand focused on simplicity, calmness, and quiet confidence.

This is not a loud beauty brand.  
This is not trendy, colorful, or aggressive.

VELUNE is:
- calm
- neutral
- premium
- effortless
- modern minimalism

The brand should feel like:
> high-end editorial skincare, not mass-market ecommerce

---

## 2. Visual Direction

### Core Style

- Minimal
- Warm neutral palette
- Soft natural lighting
- Clean compositions
- High-end product photography

Avoid:
- bright colors
- gradients
- aggressive contrast
- clutter
- "tech" feeling UI

---

### Color Palette

Primary:
- Beige / Sand (#EDE6DC, #D8CFC4)
- Warm white (#F7F5F2)

Secondary:
- Soft brown (#A89F94)
- Muted gold accents (very subtle)

Text:
- Black / Dark gray (#1A1A1A)

---

### Typography

Primary font:
- Elegant serif (e.g. Playfair, Cormorant, Canela-style)

Secondary:
- Clean sans-serif (e.g. Inter, Helvetica)

Rules:
- Serif for titles
- Sans-serif for UI / buttons / small text

Avoid:
- playful fonts
- decorative fonts
- слишком "fashion editorial" перегиб

---

## 3. Layout Principles

### Core Rules

- Air > content
- Simplicity > features
- Focus > variety

Spacing:
- Large vertical spacing
- Clean grid
- No tight layouts

---

### Structure Philosophy

Each block must have a purpose:
- Sell product
- Push subscription
- Reinforce brand

If block does not do this → remove it

---

## 4. Product Presentation

Products are the center of everything.

Rules:
- Always clean background
- Always soft shadows
- Always centered or balanced composition

Avoid:
- busy backgrounds
- props overload
- random angles

---

## 5. UX Philosophy

VELUNE UX is:

- frictionless
- predictable
- calm

User should never think.

---

### Navigation

Minimal:

- Shop
- Subscription
- Account

No dropdown chaos.

---

### Buttons

Primary:
- "Shop products"
- "Subscribe & save"

Avoid:
- "Learn more"
- "Explore"
- vague CTAs

---

## 6. Ecommerce Logic

### Purchase types:

1. One-time purchase
2. Subscription (core business)

Subscription must feel:
- simple
- beneficial
- obvious

---

### Subscription Rules

- Visible on homepage
- Highlight savings (e.g. "Save 20%")
- Easy to manage

---

## 7. Tone of Voice

Copywriting must be:

- short
- calm
- confident
- not salesy

Examples:

GOOD:
"Minimal skincare for modern skin"

BAD:
"Revolutionary anti-aging solution with powerful formula"

---

## 8. Technical Guidelines (for development)

### Stack

- WordPress (custom theme)
- WooCommerce
- Stripe (payments + subscriptions)

---

### Architecture

Core features:

- Product catalog
- Cart / Checkout
- Account system
- Subscription management

Stripe handles:
- payment methods
- recurring billing

---

## 9. What to Avoid

- Overdesign
- Too many sections
- Animations everywhere
- Loud UI
- Cheap ecommerce look

---

## 10. Decision Rule

When making any design decision:

Ask:

> "Does this make the brand feel more premium or more mass-market?"

If mass-market → reject.

---

## 11. Future Scaling

Brand must scale into:

- more products
- bundles
- subscriptions
- possible mobile app

So:
- keep system flexible
- avoid hardcoded design decisions

---

## FINAL PRINCIPLE

VELUNE is not trying to impress.  
It is trying to feel inevitable.

Everything must feel:
simple, calm, expensive.
